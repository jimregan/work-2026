We present an update to the Waxholm corpus, a phonetically annotated corpus of spoken Swedish.

The subjects are seated in an anechoic room in front of a display with a unidirectional cardioid electret condenser microphone mounted on top of it.

The subjects were recorded in an anechoic room, using a unidirectional cardioid electret condenser microphone which was mounted on top of a display used for the graphical output of the system, such as pictures, maps, charts and timetables, and, in later phases of the project, a synthesised face.
