Part of this can be taken straight from my failed interspeech 2024 submission, but there is some extra care to be taken.

One aspect of parliamentary speech generally is that there is typically a house stylesheet so that, for example, previous debates are referred to in writing according to a common standard. There are well described conventions for the Swedish Riksdag that are published — Jens mentioned that there are two books about this, I should look them up — but it’s not always about difference in convention: for example, a speaker might refer to an incorrect debate number in their speech; this will be corrected in the transcript without note.
