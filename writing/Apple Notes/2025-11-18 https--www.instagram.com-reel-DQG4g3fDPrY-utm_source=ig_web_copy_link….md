1. Go to Liljevalchs on a Monday (free entry!)
2. 'Rent' skates at Fritidsbanken en find an icerink, for example the one at Kungsträdgården
3. Relax and unwind in the beautiful garden Bergianska Trädgården
4. Enjoy the historical architecture in Gamla Stan (hopefully a snowy walk!)
5. Go to the Gingerbread house exposition at ArkDes
6. Get inspired by art at Moderna Museet on Friday between 6-8 p.m. (free entry!)
7. Try to spot a deer in Djurgården (yes it's possible!)
8. Stroll along Norr Mälarstrand (my favorite walk)
9. Watch the changing of the guard ceremony at the Royal Palace.
10. Download the map from [@stockholmsjulofficial](https://www.instagram.com/stockholmsjulofficial/) and walk along the beautiful Christmas lights in Stockholm
11. Go window shopping at Biblioteksgatan
12. Watch the iconic Christmas windows at [@nordiskakompaniet](https://www.instagram.com/nordiskakompaniet/)
13. Relax at the idyllic area 'Waldemarsudden' at Djurgården
14. Make a fire at one of the designated fireplaces or shelters around the city (you can find some nice ones in my Ultimate Stockholm Map; link in bio)
15. Go to National Museet on a Thursday between 17-20 for free entry!
16. Have a cold dip in the water!
17. Visit some beautiful art galleries in the city
18. Enjoy Skansens Christmas market (free if you have a year pass)
19. Having a 30-day SL ticket >> take a ferry ride with no extra costs
20. Have a hike in Tyresta National Park and grill some sausages
21. Slide from the snowy hills here in Stockholm
22. Go to the highest natural point in Stockholm 'Skinnarviksberget'
23. Enjoy the atmosphere at the Christmas market in Gamla Stan
24. Get inspired by the Nobel Week lights
25. Enjoy the live Christmas Calendar in Gamla Stan!
