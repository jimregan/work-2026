# Studying voice range

Follow the instructions on page 4 of the laboratory exercise (RecVox_lab.pdf) and answer the following questions.
**Speech range profiles:**
- Record SRPs with and without noise in the headphones, take screenshots of the resulting images and paste them into a document
- Compare the *Leq* and the average *fo* from the two recordings. How did they change as a result of the noise?
**Voice range profiles:**
- Record a full VRP and paste a screenshot of the resulting image into a document.
- Extract statistics for the VRP (you do not have to submit them) and discuss which parameters are relevant for the interpretation of a VRP.
- Compare the VRP and the two SRPs you created before. Discuss which parts of the voice range are used for speech.
