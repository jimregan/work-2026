Confirmation bias can be countered by adopting certain strategies. One such strategy is to actively seek out information that contradicts one's beliefs or hypotheses. This can be done by deliberately exposing oneself to diverse perspectives and sources of information. Another strategy is to engage in critical thinking and to question one's own assumptions and biases. Additionally, decision aids that require individuals to justify their decisions or consider multiple factors can reduce confirmation bias \[2\]. It is also important to be aware of the existence of confirmation bias and to consciously work to avoid it \[3\]. Finally, involving multiple individuals with diverse perspectives in decision-making processes can help to counter confirmation bias \[4\].
