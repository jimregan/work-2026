Session 5: Using the challenging questions worksheet

Reviewing CQ worksheet
Challenging assimilated stuck points
Intro. patterns of problematic thinking worksheet
Over-accommodated stuck points:

“It was my fault” — assimilated
“I always mess everything up” — over-accommodated

Patterns of problematic thinking:
lead to inaccurate & over-simplified thoughts that drive manufactured emotions
Over time, can become habitual and lead to self-defeating behaviour

Review patterns, ask which seem familiar
Categorise stuck points according to PoPT w/sheet
Consider the impact of problematic patterns or reactions to trauma and current stressors
