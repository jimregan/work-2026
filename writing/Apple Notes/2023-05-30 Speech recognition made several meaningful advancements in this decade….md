Speech recognition made several meaningful advancements in this decade. This was mostly due to the US Department of Defense and DARPA. The Speech Understanding Research (SUR) program they ran was one of the largest of its kind in the history of speech recognition. [Carnegie Mellon’s “Harpy’ speech system](https://stacks.stanford.edu/file/druid:rq916rn6924/rq916rn6924.pdf) came from this program and was capable of understanding over 1,000 words which is about the same as a three-year-old’s vocabulary.
