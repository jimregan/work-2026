In this 30% seminar, I will reprise two presentations from my work so far, which focus on the use of phoneme recognition. The first is our entry to the PSST challenge which was presented at LREC last year, focusing on the use of phoneme recognition as a diagnostic tool for aphasic speech. The second, presented at the Språkbanken workshop this year, focuses on using phoneme recognition as a means of repairing mismatches between ASR output and a reference text, as well as serving as a basis for an acoustically verified pronunciation lexicon of Swedish.
