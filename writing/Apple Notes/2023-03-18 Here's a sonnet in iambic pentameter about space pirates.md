Here's a sonnet in iambic pentameter about space pirates:

```
The space pirates sail through the galaxy,
Their ship a sleek and deadly work of art,
They plunder and they pillage with such glee,
And leave destruction in their wake, apart.

They roam the stars, a band of thieves and rogues,
And seek out treasure, gold and precious stones,
They'll take what they can get, and then they'll go,
And leave behind a trail of broken bones.

But though they're feared and hated far and wide,
The space pirates have a code they live by,
They'll never harm the innocent or hide,
And always keep their word, until they die.

So if you see them coming, don't you fear,
Just give them what they want, and they'll be clear.
```
