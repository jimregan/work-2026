Analysis of recording facilities from technical specifications

**Task**
Evaluate whether the recording facilities (including anechoic chamber, microphone, preamplifier, conditioning amplifier and sound interface) in the Phonetics Lab are suitable for voice and speech research according to the criteria and recommendations in (Sramkova, Granqvist, Herbst, & Švec, 2015; Švec & Granqvist, 2010). Use the provided technical specifications for the recording equipment (available on Athena) and comment on each of the factors addressed in the paper: (i) frequency response and range, (ii) directionality, (iii) proximity effect, (iv) dynamic range, (v) transducer type, (vi) microphone preamplifier, and (vii) digital capturing device.
If you are normally using other recording facilities than those in the Phonetic Laboratory for voice and speech research, do the same kind of evaluation for those facilities instead (collect technical specifications and evaluate against recommendations). If you identify problems, try to suggest possible improvements.
Report the result of your evaluation in a text of maximum 4000 characters including spaces. Remember that this is an individual assignment. Assignments should be submitted as .DOCX or .PDF files.
