# EGG, 13/10/2022

Large glottis, prominent thyroid cartilage = strong signal
(Opposite = weak)

EGG amplitude <-> Vertical Laryngeal Position

VLP changes VF distance to electrodes

EGG amplitude is not a reliable measure of the amount of contacting

EGG signal is
- not acoustic
- Only moderately related to loudness

Shows:

Up -> making contact
Down -> breaking contact

Shows how contact area is changing

Does not show:
- How far apart the VF are moving
- How quickly VF are moving
- If adduction becomes complete (zero flow)
- If abduction becomes complete (widest glottis)

CQ_EGG = C / T
(Contact quotient, contact, period T)

Contact time from max and min of the derivative

BUT

dEGGmin often unclear
dEGGmax sometimes has double peaks
